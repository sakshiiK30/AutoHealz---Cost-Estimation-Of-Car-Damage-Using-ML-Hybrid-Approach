1 How does AutoHealz work?

Simply upload a photo of your damaged car. Our AI system analyzes the image, detects the damaged parts, and provides an accurate repair cost estimate.

2 Who can use AutoHealz?

Any Maruti Suzuki car owner who wants to quickly check repair costs or part availability without visiting a service center can use AutoHealz. It’s simple, fast, and accessible to everyone.

3 Is AutoHealz accurate in detecting car damage?

Yes. AutoHealz uses advanced AI models trained on hundreds of Maruti Suzuki car images to ensure high accuracy in detecting and identifying damaged areas.

4 Does AutoHealz only work for Maruti Suzuki cars?

Currently, yes. AutoHealz is designed specifically for Maruti Suzuki models to ensure precise part detection and cost prediction. Future updates will include other brands as well.

1 How can I contact the AutoHealz support team?

You can reach our support team anytime at Autohealz@gmail.com or through the Contact Us form on our website. We’ll respond within 24 hours to assist you with any issues.

2 What should I do if my image upload fails?

Please check your internet connection and make sure the image size is under the allowed limit. If the problem continues, refresh the page and try again — or contact our support team for help.

3 I didn’t get my cost estimation result. What should I do?

Sometimes, poor image quality or server delays may cause this. Try re-uploading a clear photo of the damaged area. If the issue persists, reach out to us through the support section.
